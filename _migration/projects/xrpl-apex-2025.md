Project Description
"Hosted by Ripple, Apex 2025 is the official global community summit that unites developers, innovators, businesses, and investors for an exciting three-day celebration of blockchain technology and the incredible world of the XRP Ledger." - XRP Ledger Apex Website
Working alongside Dave Tennent and Trevor Grove, we created a multi-kiosk touchscreen experience for the XRP Ledger Apex conference in Singapore.
We translated Invisible North’s creative brief into a responsive system that invited users to explore a curated set of topics, express preferences, and receive a personalized persona.
The experience concluded with on-site pin that guests could wear with pride. We also facilitated a follow-up email featuring tailored content based on their preferences.
Built using TouchDesigner and deployed with real-time interactivity, the system blended playfulness with data collection to drive deeper audience engagement.
Special thanks to Dave and Sanaz, the entire CT team in Singapore, and Trevor Grove and the Invisible North team for all of the production work behind the scenes!
