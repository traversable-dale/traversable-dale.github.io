Project Description
For this project, I worked with Cam Vokey to setup and monitor the product demo room for Miro's annual Canvas conference.
The demo room contained a custom wraparound desk which housed ten Mac Minis locked in kiosk mode. The staff wanted guests to be able to use the kiosks, log into their accounts, and access their Miro boards without accessing the desktop.
In addition to the kiosks, there were four in-house projectors mounted in the space. We used MadMappper to blend and color correct, and used QLab to drive playback.
I was involved with installation, troubleshooting, and on-site production.
Special thanks to Ben Baur and Livesight!
