SHAKER MOUNTAIN
Project Description
Laser Projections + Content for Low-Res
Adirondacks, NY
May 2023
Shaker Mountain is a music festival in the Adirondacks of upstate New York that takes place every summer- created by Andrew Giugno a.k.a. Lowres
In 2023, I was invited to control the laser that would shine over the main camping area of the festival. I developed a variety of short looping animations that would play throughout the weekend. Using a combination of After Effects and MadMapper, the process was satisfying and fun.
Capturing a laser on camera never really does it justice, so you will just have imagine it looked even cooler than this!
