Project Description
The Diesel Sound of the Brave launch party was an event at Elsewhere in Brooklyn, intended for influencers and celebrities to celebrate the joy of fragrance. The Sound of the Brave!
I was involved in pre-production, pre-visualization, content creation, and installation for the event- part of the incredible team at Chemistry Creative, with stage design by Pete Sax, and V1 operations by Greg Zifcak
The main event was a stage design that included 2x LED walls and 8x large displays on either side of the stage. Throughout the night, DJs and performers would take the stage with a variety of Diesel fragrance content playing in the background.
There were also a number of smaller activations around the room, including a LED wall phot booth with a variety of backgrounds to choose from.
