Project Description
The Blue Paradox is an immersive exhibition at the Griffin Museum of Science and Industry in Chicago, IL.
Much of what’s publicly available can be found on the museum’s page, which provides a window into the experience.
The exhibition was a major collaborative effort involving designers, programmers, and production crews, and it remains one of the most memorable installations I’ve had the chance to work on. It was a massive team effort, and one I’m proud to have been a part of.
Photos were taken on-site by Ben Bauer on his nice camera, or myself with my fuzzy camera.
