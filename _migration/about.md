## Body copy (verbatim)

Hi! I'm John
I am a Creative Technologist based in NYC, specializing ininteractive multimedia installations for museums, exhibitions, and experiential spaces.
I help translate creative concepts into working systems, from early technical feasibility and 3D visualization through interactive prototyping and AV system integration.
With a background in environmental science, film, and motion graphics, I bring a unique perspective to technical problem-solving. I love figuring out how things work and turning ambitious ideas into real installations.
If you've got a project, let's talk about it!
JOHN@JOHNSYZONENKO.COM
