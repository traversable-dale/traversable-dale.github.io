Scroll Scrub

Shiny Cat — Scroll Scrub

A canvas frame-sequence scrubber (423 frames). As you scroll, the cat is pinned to the viewport and its rotation scrubs in sync with the scrollbar — sharp, and no flicker. This is the right technique for a rendered clip like this (not a Lottie flipbook).

Scroll down to reach the cat, then keep scrolling to spin it.
